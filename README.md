I build human-centred technologies focused on real-world **accessibility**. I'm currently a Research Associate at **King's College London** working on [Advokit](https://www.advokit.uk/) — an inclusive GenAI toolkit co-designed with people with aphasia to support access to public services including benefit navigation and language assistance. My PhD examined wearable and discreet AAC technologies for stroke survivors, across 4 years of participatory design with 200+ participants in disadvantaged London communities. Previously interned at **Microsoft Research, Ability Team**, designing AI-powered meeting agents for Microsoft Teams.
